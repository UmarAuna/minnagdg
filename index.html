Google Developers Group

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the Android, App Engine, and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs like the Maps API, YouTube API and Google Calendar API. GDG Minna is one of the 537 chapters running in 103 countries around the world.

We are a place to learn about Google Technologies and Tools for developers, place to see what local companies and developers are doing with these technologies and A place to meet cool and smart people in tech but most notably GDG is Focused on developers and educational technical content.
